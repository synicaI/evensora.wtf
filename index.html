FEATURE DOCUMENTATION

OKBYE.LOL script documentation

VITAL: Control Center

The core mechanics that keep the script running and secure.

  • Key System: Your personal license to access the cheat.
  • FPS Cap: Limits your frames per second (set to 0 for uncapped).
  • Unload Key: The emergency exit (default: End) to stop the script instantly.
  • Universal Mode: Toggle to attempt usage in games other than Voidfall.

HEADING: Combat & Aiming

The tools used to enhance accuracy and reaction time.

Silent Aim: Modifies bullet paths to hit targets without moving your camera. Hit chance controls consistency, prediction accounts for movement, FOV circle limits target area, and visible check avoids wall targets.

Trigger Bot: Automatically fires when an enemy crosses your reticle. Delay can be used to mimic human reaction speed.

Camlock: Locks camera movement onto a target. Smoothness reduces snapping, while jitter adds small hand-like variance.

CORE: Movement & Gun Mechanics

Physical modifications to character and weapon behavior.

  • WalkSpeed: Increases movement speed.
  • JumpPower: Increases jump height.
  • NoRecoil: Removes the upward kick of guns when firing.
  • NoSpread: Forces bullets to travel in a straight line.

SAFETY: Stealth & Protection

Features intended to reduce detection risk and preserve access.

  • Anti-Kick: Attempts to bypass automatic kick systems.
  • Whitelist: Prevents listed players from being targeted.
  • Anti-Curve: Reduces visible bullet bending patterns.

VISUALS: ESP

Overlay information to track enemy position and status.

  • Boxes: Draws frames around players.
  • Names: Displays usernames.
  • Health Bars: Shows remaining health.
  • Snaplines: Draws lines from your position to enemies.
  • Distance: Displays range in studs.

INVENTORY: Loadout Manager

Slots: Organizes weapons into hotbar positions (1-9) so your layout stays consistent in game.
