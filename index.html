FAQ

Common questions

What is Evensora and where do I get updates?

Evensora is a paid product and updates/support are provided through the official Discord server.

How do I get a key?

Join the community group, open a ticket with proof, then follow the team instructions to receive access.

How do HWID resets work?

Resets are handled by support requests and can take time depending on queue and validation.

Any rules I should know?

Do not share keys. By using the product, you agree to the terms listed in the official Discord.
