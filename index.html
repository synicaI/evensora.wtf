FAQ

Feature breakdown

What is Evensora?

Evensora is presented as a paid configuration product with a central control panel, one lifetime plan, and categorized script modules for easier setup and reading.

Vital (Key, FPS cap, unload key, universal mode)

Vital contains essential runtime settings: key field, frame cap behavior, emergency unload key, and global mode toggle.

Heading: SilentAim, TriggerBot, Camlock

This category groups aim-related options, including hit chance, target part logic, keybind activation, prediction, FOV visibility, and smoothing behavior.

Core: WalkSpeed, JumpPower, NoRecoil, NoSpread

Core covers movement and gun response values. It combines boolean toggles with numeric values for speed and jump behavior.

Safety: AntiKick, WhitelistedPlayers, AntiCurve

Safety focuses on protection controls and allowlists. WhitelistedPlayers prevents listed users from being included in targeting flows.

Inventory: Slots mapping

Inventory assigns weapon names to slots 1-9 to keep a consistent layout and predictable order in the loadout.

Visuals: Boxes, Names, HealthBars, Snaplines, Distance

Visuals combines overlay toggles, color fields, and team-check flags for display modules like boxes, names, bars, lines, and distance labels.

How do HWID resets work?

HWID resets follow a periodic schedule (typically weekly), and requests may require a valid reason plus support validation before processing.

How long can HWID reset requests take?

Processing time can vary by queue load; requests may take up to 24 hours depending on support capacity and request quality.

Can keys be shared?

No. Sharing keys is against policy and may result in access removal from related services.
